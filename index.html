About Us

Welcome to Luna's Spot, your cozy neighborhood shop for delicious milk tea and fruity sodas! Founded in 2020, we've been dedicated to serving the community with high-quality beverages made from the freshest ingredients.

Our mission is simple: to create memorable drink experiences that bring people together. Whether you're catching up with friends, studying, or just taking a break, Luna's Spot is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a refreshing beverage.

Our Cozy Shop Interior

All our drinks are crafted with care, using premium tea leaves, real fruit, and high-quality ingredients. We never use artificial flavors or preservatives, ensuring every sip is as natural and delicious as possible.
